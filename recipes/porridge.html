Porridge

Description

After a hard day of raiding and a wild night of drinking mead, what better way to start your day than with a steaming bowl of porridge?

Here's how to make a bowl of oat porridge!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 2 cups of water
  • A dash of salt
  • A dash of honey

Instructions

  1. Pour the oats, salt and water into a small pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir frequently until the water has been mostly absorbed by the oats.
  3. Plate the oats and drizzle some honey over them. Enjoy!
